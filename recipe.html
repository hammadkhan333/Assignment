Recipes: Biryani and Corn Soup

Biryani

Ingredients for Biryan

- Basmati rice - Chicken or vegetables - Spices (cumin, cardamom, cinnamon, cloves) - Onions, tomatoes - Yogurt - Ghee - Saffron - Water - Salt

Steps

1. Marinate the chicken or vegetables with yogurt and spices. 2. Cook the rice separately. 3. Layer the marinated chicken/vegetables and cooked rice. 4. Add saffron-infused water. 5. Cook until done. Serve hot! Enjoy your homemade Biryani!

Corn Soup

Ingredients for Corn Soup

- Corn kernels - Chicken or vegetable broth - Onion, garlic - Butter - Heavy cream - Salt and pepper

Steps

1. Saut√© onions and garlic in butter until translucent. 2. Add corn kernels and cook briefly. 3. Pour in chicken or vegetable broth. 4. Simmer until corn is tender. 5. Blend the soup until smooth. 6. Return to the pot, add cream, and season with salt and pepper. Serve your creamy Corn Soup hot!
